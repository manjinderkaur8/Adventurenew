Policy on Cancellation

Our travel agency's cancellation and refund policy is designed to provide transparency and clarity to our customers. By using our website and services, you agree to abide by the following cancellation and refund terms. It is important to carefully read and understand this policy before making any bookings.

Cancellation Eligibility and Refund Process:

  • Eligibility: Cancellation requests will only be accepted if made within 72 hours of the original booking.

  • Refund Process: Cancellation requests must be made over the phone with our customer service representatives. Requests made via email or chat will not be considered.

Refund Eligibility:

  • Refunds are subject to the cancellation policies of hotels, airlines, and other service providers involved in the booking. We cannot guarantee refunds for cancellations beyond the 72-hour window due to the specific rules and regulations set by these providers.

  • After the 72-hour window, cancellations are generally not eligible for a refund

Non-Refundable Bookings:

  • Some bookings may be non-refundable from the time of purchase. These non-refundable bookings will be clearly communicated to you during the booking process.

  • In the case of non-refundable bookings, no refunds will be provided for cancellations made at any time after the booking is confirmed.

Modification or Rescheduling:

  • If you wish to modify or reschedule your booking, please contact our customer service team. Changes are subject to availability and the policies of the respective service providers. Additional charges may apply based on the nature of the modification.

Force Majeure:

  • In the event of unforeseen circumstances beyond our control, such as natural disasters, pandemics, or government-imposed travel restrictions, our cancellation policy may be adjusted accordingly. We will make every effort to provide fair and reasonable solutions during such exceptional circumstances.

Acknowledgement of Policy:

  • By using our website and services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood our cancellation and refund policy.

  • Your continued use of our website indicates your acceptance of these policies. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please refrain from using our services.

Please note that this policy is subject to change without prior notice. Any updates or modifications will be posted on our website. For any further clarification or inquiries regarding our cancellation and refund policy, please contact our customer service team.
